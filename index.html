About

Medical scientist.
Now a designer.

I trained for years as a Medical Laboratory Scientist, working across chemical pathology, immunology, haematology, and histopathology. It's exacting work. One mislabelled sample and a diagnosis is wrong. That standard for precision never left me, even after I moved into design.

I got into UI/UX through DigiGirls during a university strike, and it stuck because it let me combine that same care for accuracy with something more creative: figuring out what actually makes a product work for the person using it. Since then I've designed for a healthcare research company, a SaaS product, and a nonprofit, always starting from research, not assumptions.

I think in systems. I ask why a flow feels confusing before I touch the visuals. That same curiosity is also why I recently graduated from AltSchool Africa's Cloud Engineering programme and am continuing to build in that space, I want to understand how products work end to end, not just at the interface layer.

What I bring to a team: professional research training from my background as a Medical Laboratory Scientist, a genuinely user centered approach, documentation discipline, and the ability to put myself in the user's shoes when shaping an experience.

Verify Social: Trust & Verification Platform

Verify Social is a trust focused platform built to close the gap between customers and businesses during online transactions, promoting transparency and confidence in social commerce. I've led product design on it since 2023, from early discovery through to a live product used by real businesses today.

The Problem

Online transactions between customers and small businesses often lack a reliable way to verify legitimacy on either side, leading to trust issues that slow down or block otherwise good transactions.

My Role
  • Own the end-to-end design process across the full product lifecycle, from discovery to implementation
  • Lead user research through surveys, questionnaires, and competitor analysis
  • Work directly with founders and engineers to translate research into functional, scalable experiences
  • Continuously monitor usage and feedback to guide ongoing product decisions

Impact

What started as an early concept has grown into a functional platform used by real businesses. It's reinforced a core belief I bring to every product: good design comes from continuous research, collaboration, and iteration, not assumptions.
